Cold Email vs Funnels: Why I Don’t Build Funnels Anymore

Simple systems that contact the right people and get replies. No funnels required.

Everyone online is pitching funnels. They sell ten step systems with tripwires, webinars, drip sequences, upsells, downsells, and dashboards. All of it just to get one lead in the door.

It sounds impressive until the funnel is not converting and you are out of time and money.

I do not build that. I build something better.

Cold email.

Not newsletters. Not pitch decks. Not a long video with a countdown timer. A simple message sent to the right person with the right offer. No funnel needed.

Cold email gets you in front of real buyers now. If your offer is good and your targeting is clean you will get replies.

Funnels are not bad. They are just not the first step. The first step is contact.

I work with coaches, car lots, contractors, mortgage brokers, agency owners, and any operator who needs leads and has an offer that converts when someone hears it. I build cold email systems that run daily, get replies, and book calls.

The system looks like this

  • One inbox
  • One verified lead list
  • One short sequence
  • Replies direct to your inbox

Sometimes we add a landing page or a booking link. That is optional. The core works on its own.
